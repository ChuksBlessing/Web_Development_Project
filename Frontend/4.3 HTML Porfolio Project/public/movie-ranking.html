Suits follows Harvey Specter, a senior partner at a top New York law firm, who hires Mike Ross, a brilliant but unlicensed young lawyer who has a photographic memory. The Last Ship follows the crew of the USS Nathan James, a US Navy destroyer that is at sea when a global pandemic kills over 80% of the world's population. The crew must race against time to find a cure and save humanity.
